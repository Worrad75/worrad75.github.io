My Story



Who Am I?

I'm Darrow, a 26-year old web developer from New York City. I've been building full-stack applications for almost 4 years now, and I've had the opportunity to work with a variety of frameworks and technologies across different employers and projects. I've always had an appreciation for computers, so the day that I realized coding was basically just talking to one, I was hooked.

How Did I Get Here?

In 2019, I graduated from Brandeis University with a Bachelors of Arts in African and Afro-American Studies, and a Minor in Economics. I've always tried to be cognisant of the impact I have on the world and the people around me, and my schooling reinforced for me the importance of reaching outside your comfort zone to obtain new perspectives; a problem that has been solved from only 1 point of view has not actually been solved.


Right out of college, I knew I wanted to become a developer, so I joined Flatiron School. There, I learned what I meant to work with websites, progressing from Ruby on Rails and SQL to Javascript and eventually React and Redux. At the start of 2020, I joined Infosys Limited as a Software Engineer, and I was fortunate to work for their client UBS, an international bank. In addition to the incredible people I met during my time there, what I took most from it was a deeper understanding of how finiancial technologies were built, maintained, and revamped over time.


Midway through 2021, I joined Attentive Mobile as a Solutions Engineer at the suggestion of a friend. What I found was a open and caring team of developers with similar stories to mine and a willingness to teach me how to translate my rather unrefined skills into production-level craftsman tools. With greater responsibility came greater understanding of the tangible connectivity and the intricate balancing act that is maintaining a substantial codebase.

What Else Do I Do?

In my personal time, I love spending time with my friends and family. I'm a playfully competitive person, so video games, card games, board games, and sports are all near and dear to my heart. I never pass up a chance to go skateboarding (or snowboarding if the weather is right!). My secret talent is that I take salsa classes every week, and I make it a habit to build my own furniture whenever possible.
